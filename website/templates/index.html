Welcome to Green Plan-It

This is Green Plan-It, a tool that is allows you to estimate the yearly enery usage for a structure. This model was created using data from the 2009 Residential Energy Consumption Survey compiled by the U.S. Energy Information Administration. To assist in responding to the questions a sample structure is provided. To estimate the yearly energy usage download the CSV found below, and change the values from the default. The range of applicability is:

  • 100 to 16,000 sq ft
  • Must be in the US
  • Cannot be used to model stuctures that heat/cool a seconday structure (i.e. barn)
